## Full description (max 4000 chars)

```text
ProbWin AI is an analytical app for football fans that shows the top 10 most important matches every day, with detailed statistics and AI-generated commentary.

THE PROBLEM WITH SPORT ANALYTICS
================================

Bookmakers price every match down to the percent. Sport apps (FlashScore, Sofascore) show scores but rarely EXPLAIN why an odds line is set the way it is. ProbWin AI fills that gap.

WHAT THE APP SHOWS
==================

For each of the top 10 matches of the day:

* ELO RATINGS for both teams (source: Clubelo.com)
* FORM over the last 5 matches (W/D/L)
* GOAL STATISTICS (scored/conceded, averages)
* H2H - recent head-to-head results
* BOOKMAKER ODDS 1/X/2 (source: public aggregators)
* MATHEMATICAL PROBABILITIES computed by Elo model
* AI COMMENTARY in plain language - explaining context, injuries, motivation

FROM A FAN TO A FAN
===================

ProbWin AI is NOT a betting advisor. We don't promise profits. The app is an informational tool for people who want to better understand the sport.

Validation of the mathematical model across 3 Premier League seasons showed that public statistical data (Elo, form, H2H) is insufficient to beat the bookmaker. Bookmakers have an informational advantage (injuries, lineups, sharp money). We show these numbers honestly - so you can interpret them yourself.

FEATURES
========

* Daily refresh of top 10 matches from major leagues (Premier League, La Liga, Bundesliga, Serie A, Ligue 1)
* Detailed match card: statistics + AI commentary + model vs bookmaker comparison
* Dark mode (easy on the eyes for late-evening analysis)
* Off-line cache - recently fetched matches available without internet
* No ads, no betting push notifications, no shady operators
```
